             When Calibre Carriers was formed in 2004 our core business was firmly rooted in Containarised Trucking by road transport operations. Development and expansion quickly followed. Our groupage operations proved immensely successful thanks to reliable regular departures and arrivals and critically our ability to engender and retain customer loyalty.

These operations have dovetailed very effectively with the Company’s well established Trucking from Chennai to down south, especially from Chennai to Bangalore, Pondicherry and all over Tamilnadu, offering time and cost efficient solutions for Exporters, Importers, manufactures, Freight forwarders, Custom House Agents and for other type of Industries. Calibre Carriers is a proprietorship concern, managed and operated through highly skilled and trained workforce. Calibre Carriers specializes in bonded trucking of Air cargo shipments and Sea cargo shipments.
